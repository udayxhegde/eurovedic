Hi, my name is Kavita. I love the outdoors, good food and travelling. But most of all, I have loved working as an esthetician. From a very young age, I have worked with my mother back in India in her spa. Now I enjoy being a SPA consultant, helping others to setup a perfect SPA they can be proud about.

I am a licensed esthetician in the state of Washington and I have been working in Washington since 2006. Recently, I have started my own consulting service for SPA. My specialities are in SPA design as well as picking the right SPA services that delight your customers.

After moving to Washington, I was fortunate enough to be educated at the best European holistic school. Learning different approaches of taking care of skin has become my passion.

Having worked for more than 25 years in this industry, I have learnt that no skin is like another, each needing its own specialized care. Mixing the latest technology along with ancient methods can do wonders to our skin.
